The journey to create Made in Vietnam Lean Management Technology

  • GKM Vietnam
  • Jan 1, 2022

The civilized prosperity of a country is measured by the quality of products and services that its people create. Each country, or in other words, all businesses of that country, need to rely on their strengths, develop high-class key products and services, replace imported goods, and gradually export to to sustainable and lasting development. Keeping in his burning desire to help the country develop, Associate Professor, Dr. Nguyen Dang Minh founded "Lean Management Technology Made in Vietnam" as a solution to realize that dream. The following is the journey of creating and spreading Technology to thousands of Vietnamese businesses and organizations.

Witnessing the life cycle of Vietnamese businesses being too short, each generation is born, develops and dies without having time to learn lessons for the next generation. He always wonders why Vietnamese people are smart and good at their jobs, but when they start a business, productivity decreases and quality is not as expected. After studying and researching, he realized that the problem Vietnamese people are facing is not about technology or profession but about management.

In 1998, after graduating from Hanoi University of Science and Technology, he received a scholarship to Japan, where he spent time approaching the most elite places in terms of profession and management to later bring back to help businesses and organizations. Vietnamese officials. The process of living and working in Japan also helps me understand the Japanese method of creating "elite" and "neat" through every daily task and inside Japanese products and services. During his time working at the Toyota Group while studying and researching his PhD at Nayoga University, he discovered the real problem of governance in the country.

Importing technology and science and technology can be as effective as the original, but importing management solutions from the world is not effective because it is not suitable for Vietnamese culture and human identity. . Therefore, each country and each people must find and perfect their own governance solutions for their own country and people.

In 2011, he decided to return home to directly participate in solving practical problems of more than 300 Vietnamese businesses. Understanding each business's disease and then drawing and perfecting the solution set, which is a management technology independent of technical technology and vocational technology, applicable to all Vietnamese individuals and organizations. any structure, scale, field to improve productivity and profits.

Made in Vietnam Lean Management Technology is a management technology built on the aspirations of Vietnamese people, for Vietnamese people. Helping Vietnamese people, products and services become more refined and neater, thereby constantly creating profits and added value, contributing to the sustainable development of the country and humanity.

In the first days when Teacher proposed this solution, Vietnamese businessmen did not pay much attention, they still preferred to choose the "Made in USA" (USA) or "Made in Japan" (Japan) management system instead. because "Made in Vietnam". However, the desire to help society develop is the driving force that helps him persevere in spreading this Pure Vietnamese Management Technology to each business and organization. Reality has proven that thousands of businesses have been effectively applying partial or complete solutions of "Lean Management Technology Made in Vietnam".

Not stopping there, he also has a greater aspiration, spreading management technology to every family, business, school and the entire society for a prosperous and powerful Vietnam future.
