  • Opening Chapter Lean Management Thinking “Made in Viet Nam”, Mind The World Category, Mapping Model

    In the opening chapter, the author provides readers with the most basic knowledge about "Made in Vietnam" Lean Management Technology, Tam The Management Category and Mapping Model.   • Chapter 1 Overview of lean management

    At the end of chapter 1, readers will have a general overview of lean management (concepts, tools and application methods) and will understand the foundation of Made in Vietnam Lean Management thinking. Male

  • Chapter 2 International experience applying lean management – Implications for Vietnam

    In this chapter, readers will learn about experiences applying lean management in some typical countries around the world such as the US, Japan, Singapore... Through that, the author and readers will draw lessons. experience and map it into your business practice

  • Chapter 3The current status of applying Lean Management in organizations Vietnamese businesses

    Chapter 3 provides readers with a comprehensive picture of the current status of implementing lean management in some typical enterprises. Combined with the lessons learned in chapter two, reading will have a more practical mapping into planning, and a clearer direction in applying lean management to business practice. your organization.

  • Chapter 4 Practical application of lean management in Vietnam Nam

    Chapter 4 will provide evidence of typical businesses applying lean management. Readers will find for themselves specific methods and practical experiences in applying Lean Management from many different angles.

  • Chapter 5 Model of applying lean management at Enterprise Vietnamese industry

    Chapter 5 will provide readers with a number of lean management implementation models in Vietnam, which are the results of mapping practical research in Vietnam to create the Made in Vietnam Lean Management model.
